# Pilot Limitations

- Local LM Studio is required for all AI workflows.
- No cloud LLM fallback is allowed.
- No mock LLM output or fake AI output is allowed.
- No voice or video interviews are implemented.
- No coding assessment is implemented.
- No LinkedIn scraping is implemented.
- No job board integration is implemented.
- The product is not a full ATS replacement.
- AI outputs are advisory.
- Human approval is required for criteria, shortlist decisions, and final hiring decisions.
- Eval fixtures are synthetic and do not prove production performance.
- The pilot should avoid high-stakes automated decisions.
- Candidate consent is required for interviews.
- Protected attributes must not be used for scoring, interview questions, risks, or recommendations.
- The scorecard is evidence-based but is not legally or compliance certified.
- Browser print is the supported pilot scorecard export path.
- Candidate live chat uses a single browser session nonce; lost sessions may require a new invite.

Final scorecard disclaimer:

> AI-generated recommendations are advisory. A qualified human reviewer must make the final hiring decision.

# Code Of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We aim to make participation in this project respectful, constructive, and harassment-free for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and professional.
- Assume good intent while staying precise about technical issues.
- Accept constructive feedback.
- Focus disagreements on code, design, documentation, and project goals.
- Respect privacy and do not share sensitive candidate, security, or personal data.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, threats, or discriminatory language.
- Public disclosure of private security issues, secrets, candidate data, or personal information.
- Personal attacks or sustained disruption.
- Spam or off-topic promotion.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, reject contributions, or block participants who violate this code of conduct. Report concerns to the project maintainer through the safest available private channel.
